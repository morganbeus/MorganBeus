Background:

My name is Morgan Beus and I was raised in the greatest place on earth: Mesa, Arizona! I am a desert girl at heart and I love spending time in the sun. I spend my time going to school, working, and socializing. I am the youngest of six children and the aunt of 7 nieces and nephews. I believe hard work is important and I love to learn new things. I love to spend time with my family and friends. Someday I want to be an attorney in order to help bring greater peace into the lives of those around me.
